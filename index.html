Dopamine
(The reward chemical)

Try to avoid things that trigger huge spikes in dopamine (Junk food,drugs etc.)

Healthy ways to boots it:
  • 3-5 minute cold shower
  • Eat a high protein diet
  • Get early morning sunlight
  • Proritize quality sleep


Serotonin
(The mood stabilizer)

Lack of serotonin can lead to increased feeling of anxiety and depression.

Healthy ways to boots it:
  • 20-30 minutes of sun exposure
  • Daily meditation practice
  • Keep a gratitude journal
  • Spend time in nature


Oxytocin
(The love hormone)

Oxytocin builds trust and strength in relationships.

Healthy ways to boots it:
  • Give a hug
  • Give/Receive a gift
  • Random acts of kindness
  • Speak with a loved one


Endorphins
(The pain reliever)

Endorphins can reduce your perception of pain and give feelings of euphoria.

Healthy ways to boots it:
  • Watch comedy(laughter)
  • Moderate exercise
  • Eat dark chocolate
  • Aromatherapy
